## 💰 The Formula

```
Confidence = (Earnings × 0.40) + (Hiring × 0.35) + (Volume × 0.25)
```

### Earnings (40% weight)
- ✅ EPS beat last quarter
- ✅ Forward guidance increase
- ❌ Missed expectations = lower score

### Hiring (35% weight)
- ✅ Job postings up >10%
- ✅ Expansion into new markets
- ❌ Hiring freeze = lower score

### Volume (25% weight)
- ✅ Trading volume >1.5x average
- ✅ Positive price momentum
- ❌ Declining volume = lower score
